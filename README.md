# AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System

## Project Overview

The AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS) is a Salesforce-based recruitment platform developed to automate and simplify the complete hiring process. The system enables recruiters and hiring managers to manage job openings, candidate profiles, candidate applications, approval workflows, and AI-assisted hiring recommendations from a centralized Salesforce environment.

The project is developed using Salesforce declarative tools without custom Apex programming. It integrates automation, approval workflows, reporting, dashboards, and Agentforce AI to improve recruitment efficiency and decision-making.

## Project Modules

### Candidate Management
Maintains candidate information including contact details and recruitment status.

### Job Opening Management
Stores information about available job openings, departments, salary ranges, and vacancies.

### Candidate Application
Tracks candidate applications, AI scores, approval status, offer amount, and application status.

### Workflow Automation
Automates recruitment processes using Salesforce Flows.

### Approval Process
Routes candidate offers for managerial approval before final hiring decisions.

### Reporting & Dashboard
Provides visual reports and dashboards for recruitment monitoring.

### Agentforce AI
Provides AI-assisted recruiter guidance, candidate summaries, and recruitment recommendations.
